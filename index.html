Alokin Cocoon 2.0

Alokin is happy to announce that we have initiated Alokin Specific internal training program, “Alokin Cocoon”. Freshers who have completed their course (details below) will be considered for this program. Our aim is to assess your strengths and build your skills in multiple (technical & non-technical) areas. The duration of the Alokin Cocoon training will be for around 3 months. After the specific training you will be given the opportunity to work in Alokin on 6 months probation. During the training & probation period the candidate will be paid a monthly CTC of Rs.25,000/-. Post completion of probation, the candidate will be given a raise to 3.6 LPA.

BTech in IT or Computer Science/MCA/MTech in IT or Computer Science/MSc Software Engineering

Willing to work from Alokin Office, Technopark, Trivandrum

Last Date to Register is April 28th 2022

We prefer candidates who have completed their courses (Backlogs are not an issue)

Looking for candidates who can stay and grow with Alokin for minimum two years

Rising Stars by Cirrus Labs.(Software Trainee)

Rising Stars will undergo training on one programming language (Java / C++ / Python), fundamentals of one cloud platform (Azure / AWS / GCP), Agile Scrum model of software delivery, DevOps tools, and Accelerators. The training will be a mix of classroom sessions conducted by experts and self-learning mentored by senior professionals in CirrusLabs. Additional specialized training on certain niche tools will also be provided based on Technology / Business requirements. They will get an opportunity to work with some of the best developers and architects in the industry and build Digital Solutions across cutting-edge technologies.

Post 6 months of Internship / Training Candidates will be hired based on performance with the offer 4L / PA.
